    How to use BeerTinder!

    It's highly recommended that you "create a profile" so that all the features work properly!

    The objective of BeerTinder is to find the perfect beer for the perfect you!

    Features:

    1. Once the screen is fully loaded, you will see a selection of randomized beers.
    2. It's highly recommended that you "Create a Profile" so that you have access to all the features and storage of your activities.
    3. For each beer selection you have the option to "Like" or "Dislike".
    4. "Liking" a beer adds that beer to your history of liked beers.
    5. Each beer will have an info option that will display relevant info about the beer that is currently being shown.
    6. By clicking "where can i meet you" you will see locations on google maps of where you can get the beer.
